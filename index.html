Project Overview

This project explores the application of artificial intelligence techniques in the context of managerial economics as part of my AI in Economics course. The focus is on developing practical AI solutions that address real-world business challenges.

The project combines theoretical understanding with hands-on implementation, demonstrating proficiency in AI methodologies and their strategic applications in business decision-making.

Key areas covered include machine learning algorithms, data analysis, predictive modeling, and the ethical implications of AI in business contexts.
